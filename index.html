Smart Symptom Checker

Answer 3 quick questions and get a clear, colour-coded care recommendation instantly.

Why this matters

Many patients don't know whether to rest at home, visit a clinic, or head straight to the ER. This AI-guided triage helps you decide quickly — without needing to call anyone first.

It uses symptom patterns and severity to give a colour-coded recommendation in under 2 minutes.
